How To Play

  • Guess the Word in 6 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Examples

  • Blue color indicates that the word contains the letter but it is in the wrong spot.
  • Green color indicates that the letter is at the correct position in the word.
  • If the letter is not highlighted it means that the word does not contain the guessed letter.
